About

We at Gamer Zone are a small team dedicated to keeping peoples love of Retro Games alive. The team have a long history with gaming growing up and have made it their mission to help new and old fans of retro games.

The teams extensive history has led them to fall in love with many different game series such as Pokemon, Resident Evil, Metal Gear Solid and many more.
